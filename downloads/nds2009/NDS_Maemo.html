Nokia Developer Summit – Maemo 5 Fremantle

Nokia's latest Maemo device also received some quick spotlight during the Summit. The main highlights are OMAP3 support, high definition camera, finger-optimized UI, WVGA touch screen, HSPA data connection plus WLAN and OpenGL ES 2.0.

The most interesting possibility to developers though may be the new desktop widgets that are being made available on Maemo 5 allowing developers to easily provide home screen widgets. By the way, Maemo 5 Beta SDK is already available for download and the APIs are pretty frozen making it a good time to start testing it for early adopters.

Maemo 5 will still run on Hildon and GTK but Qt support can be downloaded separately as it is today with previous versions of Maemo. Native Qt support will come some non-specified time in the future.
